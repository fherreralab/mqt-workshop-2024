MQT 2024 will be held at Dreams Hotel in the beautiful city of Puerto Varas, located at the southern end of Lake Llanquihue. The venue is only a short taxi trip from El Tepual International Airport (PMC) in Puerto Montt. Puerto Varas is a busy tourist destination in December. Participants are advised to make reservations with sufficient anticipation in any of the many lodges, cabins, hotels and hostals in the area.

Flight Information

  • Direct flights from Santiago (SCL) to Puerto Montt (PMC) are available several times a day via JetSmart, Sky Airlines and Latam Airlines. Taxi and van rides to Puerto Varas (~20 min trip) can be booked directly at El Tepual Airport.
