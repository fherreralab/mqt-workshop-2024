Venue: Park Inn by Raddison

MQT 2024 will be held at Park Inn by Raddison in the beautiful city of Puerto Varas, located at the southern end of Lake Llanquihue. The venue is only a short taxi trip from El Tepual International Airport (PMC) in Puerto Montt. Puerto Varas is a busy tourist destination in December. Participants are advised to make reservations with sufficient anticipation in any of the many lodges, cabins, hotels and hostals in the area.

Park Inn Hotel Address: La Paz 471, 5550000 Puerto Varas, Los Lagos.

Flight Information

  • Direct flights from Santiago (SCL) to Puerto Montt (PMC) are available several times a day via JetSmart, Sky Airlines and Latam Airlines. Taxi and van rides to Puerto Varas (~25 min trip) can be booked directly at El Tepual Airport or requested at the hotel venue.
