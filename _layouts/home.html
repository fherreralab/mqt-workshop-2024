3rd Workshop on Molecular Quantum Technology MQT 2024

December 16-20, 2024. Puerto Varas, Chile.

MQT 2024 is an in-person workshop that will bring together scientists across disciplines to discuss fundamental science and applications where molecules and molecular materials could contribute to the field of quantum technology in novel ways, as well as closely related topics.

The previous version of this workshop was held in 2022 (http://mqt2022.org), attracting a very engaging group of renowned scientists and early career researchers across physics, chemistry and materials science, with a common interest in this emerging area of interdisciplinary quantum science.

Workshop Venue

  • MQT 2024 will be held at Cormoran de Las Rocas, located in the beautiful lake city of Puerto Varas.
