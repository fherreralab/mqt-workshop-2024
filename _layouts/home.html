3rd Workshop on Molecular Quantum Technology - MQT 2024

December 16-20, 2024. Puerto Varas, Chile.

MQT 2024 is a unique workshop that brings together researchers across disciplines to discuss fundamental science and applications of molecules and molecular materials for next-generation quantum technology.

Previous MQT workshops have attracted an engaging group of renowned scientists and early career researchers from physics, chemistry and materials science, with common interests in this growing area of quantum science. Contributions from students and postdocs are strongly encouraged.

The Venue

  • MQT 2024 will be held at Park Inn by Raddison in the beautiful lake city of Puerto Varas, the northern entry point of the Chilean patagonia.

Important Dates:

  • Poster submission deadline: October 25th, 2024.
  • Registration deadline: November 11th, 2024.
